# 60-Day AI Application Developer Tracker & Strategy

## Goal Statement
Transition from a BPO background to an AI Application Developer role in a Bengaluru startup. This repository will serve as my learning workspace and public portfolio.

---

## How to Use This Plan
- **Assumptions:** 1–2 focused hours per day. Aiming for AI app/LLM developer roles in Bengaluru startups.
- **Structure:**
  - **Weeks 1–2:** Strong dev + LLM basics.
  - **Weeks 3–4:** One real end‑to‑end LLM/RAG app you can show in interviews.
  - **Weeks 5–8:** Bigger project, polish, interviews, networking, job applications.

---

## Weeks 5–8: Bigger Project & Live Job Hunt
**Goal:** Ship a "flagship" project, polish everything, and aggressively apply to Bengaluru AI startups.

### Weekly Pattern (Mon-Sun)
- **Mon–Thu (Study/Build):** 60–90 min on big project. 30–45 min on interview prep.
- **Fri (Applications):** Apply to 3–5 AI roles. Send 2–3 cold emails.
- **Sat–Sun (Polish):** Record demo videos. Improve docs. Read case studies.

### Job Hunting Routine (Bengaluru Focus)
- **Daily (20-30 mins):** 
  - Apply to 2-3 matching roles (LinkedIn, Wellfound, Instahyre, Cutshort).
  - Tailor resume bullets.
  - Connect with founders/engineers.
- **Weekly:**
  - Review responses and adjust profile based on market demands (e.g., FastAPI, RAG, etc.).

### Cold Email Strategy
- Target founders/CTOs for early-stage startups, and Head of Engineering for larger ones.
- **Format:** Short, mention their product, link your relevant project, ask for a 15-min call.
- **Follow-up:** 5-7 days later with a short bump.
